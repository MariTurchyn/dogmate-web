About Us

DogMate is dedicated to providing the best dog walking and sitting services. Our platform connects dog owners with trusted professionals, ensuring that your furry friends are always in safe hands.

Our mission is to create a community where dog owners can find reliable dog walkers and sitters who love and care for dogs just as much as they do. We use advanced technology to track dog walks and provide peace of mind to our users.
